Questions

Things people ask.

Do I need technical knowledge to use this? +
No. We handle the full setup and give you a simple dashboard to monitor. You only need to review content once a week if you want — or not at all.
What does "self-hosted" mean and why does it matter? +
Self-hosted means the automation runs on your own server (like Oracle Cloud Free Tier — genuinely free), not on a SaaS platform you pay monthly for. You own the infrastructure. There's no vendor lock-in and no recurring tool costs.
What platforms do you support? +
Currently X (Twitter), Instagram and LinkedIn. We're also building workflows for TikTok and Threads. Starter plans cover one platform; Growth and above cover multiple.
Will the content sound robotic or AI-generated? +
We spend a lot of time on voice and tone calibration. The system is trained on your brand's style, examples you like and specific dos/don'ts. Most people can't tell the difference — and we review with you before going live.
Is this a subscription or a one-time fee? +
One-time setup fee. You own the system. There are no monthly retainers from us — the only ongoing cost is your server (which is free on Oracle Cloud) and any API calls (typically a few euros a month).
