What is Meowlands?

Meowlands is a soulful NFT project built on Solana — blending abstract feline art with real-world purpose.

Core Concept

Meowlands is a collection of 1/1 abstract feline NFTs. Each piece expresses a unique emotional state or archetype through color, form, and texture. These aren’t profile pics — they’re portals of feline spirit.

Blockchain

Deployed on Solana for its speed, eco-friendliness and low fees. Minting and trading is done via Tensor.

Art Style

Heavily inspired by abstract expressionism and digital surrealism. All pieces are original and created to evoke curiosity, stillness or mystery.

Utility

  • 20% of profits go to real animal shelters
  • Collectors gain early access to future drops
  • Private holder Discord space

Why Meowlands?

We believe NFTs should mean something. Meowlands bridges crypto, art and compassion — with a purpose behind every piece.
