What is Meowlands?

Meowlands is an NFT collection of 1/1 abstract feline art on Solana, created with soul and purpose. 20% of all proceeds support real-world animal shelters.

How do I mint or buy a Meowland NFT?

All NFTs are listed on Tensor. You can buy directly from our main website or at: https://www.tensor.trade/trade/meowlands

What are the benefits for holders?

Early access to future drops, community-exclusive rewards, and a token-gated Discord space for collectors.
