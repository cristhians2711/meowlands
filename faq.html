❓ FAQ

What is Meowlands?

Meowlands is a collection of 1/1 abstract feline NFTs on Solana. Each piece is crafted with emotional depth and artistic soul — while supporting real animal shelters.

Where can I mint or buy?

You can mint or trade directly on Tensor.

What’s the utility?

  • 20% of profits donated to animal shelters
  • Early access to future drops
  • Private holder Discord community
  • Emotional & artistic connection to each piece

Do I need a wallet to mint?

Yes. We recommend using a Solana-compatible wallet like Phantom or Backpack. Once connected to Tensor, you can mint or purchase easily.

How can I contact the team?

Reach out via Twitter DMs or join our Discord to talk directly with the mods.
